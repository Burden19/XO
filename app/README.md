# X-O Tournament

This is a classic Tic-Tac-Toe game for Android, built with a tournament-style twist. Play against a friend on the same device, track your stats, and compete to become the tournament champion.

## Features

*   **Player-vs-Player Gameplay**: Enjoy a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe with a friend on a single device.
*   **Tournament Mode**: Compete in a "best of" series with a configurable number of games (3, 5, 7, 10, 15, or 20).
*   **Coin Flip**: A 3D coin flip animation determines which player starts the tournament, ensuring a fair and exciting start.
*   **Statistics Tracking**: Keep track of your performance with detailed stats, including win/loss streaks, win percentage, and total tournaments played.
*   **Game History**: Review your past tournaments and see how you've improved over time.
*   **Sound Effects**: Enjoy a more immersive experience with sound effects for every move, win, and draw.
*   **Customizable UI**: A clean, modern UI with a cool blue and red color scheme, and a visually appealing grid.

## How to Play

1.  **Select Your Symbol**: Choose to play as either "X" or "O".
2.  **Choose the Number of Games**: Select the number of games for your tournament.
3.  **Start the Tournament**: Click the "Play" button to start the coin flip and begin the tournament.
4.  **Play**: Take turns placing your symbol on the grid. The first player to get three in a row wins the round.
5.  **Next Game**: After each round, the winner starts the next one. If there's a draw, the starting player alternates.
6.  **Tournament Over**: At the end of the tournament, the player with the most wins is declared the champion.

## Setup

1.  Clone the repository to your local machine.
2.  Open the project in Android Studio.
3.  Build and run the app on an Android device or emulator.
